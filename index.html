About Me

I'm a Physics Msc degree student enrolled in the University of Coimbra. I'm pationate about understanding the fundamental laws that govern our universe or the models that we can devise to describe it. I find it fascinating how it is possible for us to create mathematical and physical models that represent a variety of systems at different scales. In physics, I found a special interest in optics, quantum physics, condensed matter physics and statistical physics. My goal is to expand my knowledge on these topics and learn mathematical tools that will allow me to study a variety of systems, in particular, biological ones.

As a hobby, I enjoy solving programming puzzles while learning and implementing algorithms. In the future, I hope to marry my interests in programming, biology and physics to create and implement mathematical models of biological systems.
